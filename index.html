Our Value Proposition

We offer innovative solutions that provide key territorial information efficiently and accessibly:

  • Territorial Evolution Reports: Detailed analysis with downloadable maps (PDF, GeoTIFF) to understand land use changes.
  • Interactive WebGIS Viewer: Intuitive online platform to visualize and analyze geospatial data without specialized software.
  • Accessible Geospatial Intelligence: AI-processed data, easy to understand and use, even without technical GIS expertise.
  • AI-Driven Analysis: Precise detection of changes and land use classification using machine learning on satellite imagery.
  • 100% Online Solution: Access information from any browser, facilitating collaboration and remote analysis.

Our Clients

Our solutions are designed for:

  • Local Governments and Public Entities: For urban planning, land management, change control, and regulatory justification.
  • Urban Planners and Architects: For visualizing project areas, context analysis, and informed decision-making in projects.
  • Environmental Consultants: For impact studies, risk analysis, and monitoring environmental changes.
  • Construction and Real Estate Companies: For identifying opportunities, market analysis, and evaluating territorial development.
